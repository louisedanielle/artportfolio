    #createforearthchallenge

    #createforearthchallenge Apr 2021

    To spread awareness for Earth Day, other artists and I initiated a creative art campaign on social media, inviting participants to express their passion for the planet through various artistic prompts. Each day of that week, I shared a different theme, encouraging individuals to create and share their art using the hashtag #createforearthchallenge. This initiative not only fostered creativity but also sparked conversations about environmental issues, inspiring a community of advocates dedicated to protecting our Earth. By showcasing the diverse perspectives of artists, we aimed to engage a broader audience and motivate collective action for a sustainable future. Follow along @elle.essquisser to see the incredible contributions and join the movement!
