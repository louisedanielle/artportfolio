    The Small Moments Oct 2023

    This painting is a structural psychedelic painting of a quotational piece. This work represents graphics or visual displays related to the quote of enjoy every moment or inspired by psychedelic experiences and hallucinations known to follow the ingestion of psychedelic colors and filling. The light house visual represents the light a light house gives to those who enjoys the moment from the top of the view. These are times when you are uncertain about the future, and hence, we need to live in the present and not think about the future. Life is all about living and enjoying those small moments. It's the small moments that make life more fulfilling.

    The Walls of UPHC Sept 2023

    This Painting is something of a memorable experience of UPHC and what is more memorable than the halls of UPHC itself that we passed through every day. This is a representative of an experience with a little of contrast and depth put into making piece. I made this painting to represent the feeling of walking down the halls of UPHC. The crowded stairway during moving class and the packed halls during recess. This piece talks about the feeling of closure when everything stops and it's just her standing down the memorable halls of UPHC. I used depth of the walls to represent anxiety as she stood there alone thinking about more than just the crowded space. The painting only oriented on one main person because it told a convey story of how even on a crowded space, she will always face her school days alone.

    Hanami 2022

    This artwork is known as sakura in Japanese, cherry blossoms are the flower of the genus Prunus trees. They can symbolize the fleeting nature of life, fresh starts and even people coming together in friendship. Hana means flower, and mi means view. During the blossoming season, families and friends gather for hanami, where they eat and drink under the blossoms.
